The Wisdom of the Crowd

Yesterday watched this BBC"s video about how precise the average estimation made by a number of people can be. They call this "phenomenon" The wisdom of the crowd. Is quite cool, isn"t it?

I"d love to make a fast and simple experiment, and you can help me. 

Go to http://feiss.be/exp/crowdwisdom and ask a simple question: How many sticks do you estimate there are in the image?

When I get some hundreds of answers I"ll post the result. Let"s see how wise is the crowd! :)
