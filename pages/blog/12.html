MB - Exit from mansion

I"ve updated my web with last shot I"ve done for Maldito Bastardo! (Damn Bastard!).

This involved a landscape replacement and turning the footage into night. The latter was preety easy with color corrections, but the former was a bit tricky because in the original footage the character is in front of large bushes and trees, and his silouette is not clear at all.

I started with traditional After Effects masking, but after 4 frames it became a vertex nightmare (the silouette changes a lot) and the feathering didn"t worked at all (one arm is motion blurred, the other one is not.. same with legs... and above all, the guy has got long hair.. ), so the faster (?) and best way to do it was painting a mask frame by frame in Photoshop.

Was a hard work, indeed. To paint the motion blur, preserve the same silouette along adjacent frames without much flickering.. Photoshop CS3"s animation/video features and timeline turned out to be really really handy!!

Anyway, The result it"s not perfect at all, it still flickers a bit, but it"s a very short shot, doesn"t deserve more time.. :P
