Categorical Perception Test

Part 1: Labelling

In the cassification (labelling) test presented here, you will hear synthetic CV syllabes. Acoustically their structures are somewhat stylized. They may therefore seem a bit unnatural, but you should disregard that and just determine which of the syllabes "ba", "da" or "ga", they most resemble and then press the relevant button. About 2 seconds after you have pressed the button, the next sound will be presented. There is a count below the buttons telling you how many test sounds are left.

When you have completed the test, your results will be presented in the form of a diagram. Press the "Start" button to begin the test.
